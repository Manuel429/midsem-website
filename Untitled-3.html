Admissions

At Christ College, we believe in providing equal opportunities to all students who aspire to achieve academic excellence. Our admission process is designed to be simple and accessible to students from diverse backgrounds.

Why Choose Christ College?

Christ College is known for its commitment to academic excellence, character development, and spiritual growth. We offer a range of programs designed to prepare students for future success, both academically and professionally.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Official academic transcripts
  • Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)
  • Letter of recommendation from a teacher or mentor
  • Personal statement outlining academic and career goals
  • Application fee payment
  • Interview (for select programs)

How to Apply

We offer a straightforward application process:

  1. Visit our Admissions Portal to access the application form.
  2. Complete the form with accurate details.
  3. Upload the necessary documents, including transcripts and recommendation letters.
  4. Submit your application and pay the required application fee.
  5. Schedule an interview (if required for your program).
  6. Wait for your admission decision via email.

Application Deadlines

We have multiple admission cycles throughout the year. Please check our official website for the latest deadline information.

Fee Payment Methods

Christ College offers multiple methods to make the fee payment process as convenient as possible. You can pay your school fees using any of the following methods:

  • Online Bank Transfer: Pay through any of our supported banks. You will receive payment confirmation once the transfer is complete.
  • Credit/Debit Card: We accept Visa, MasterCard, and other major card providers for online payments.
  • Mobile Money: Pay via mobile money platforms like MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, and AirtelTigo Money.
  • In-Person Payment: For those who prefer to pay in cash, payments can be made directly at the campus finance office.

Fee Structure

The fee structure varies by program, and we offer flexible payment plans to suit your needs. Below is a brief overview:

Program Fee (GHS) Payment Plan
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science GHS 5,000 Full Payment / Two Installments
Bachelor of Arts in English GHS 4,500 Full Payment / Three Installments
Bachelor of Business Administration GHS 6,000 Full Payment / Four Installments
Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering GHS 6,500 Full Payment / Two Installments

Important Payment Dates

To ensure smooth processing of your ward’s registration and academic activities, please adhere to the following important payment dates:

  • First Semester Fee Deadline: 30th June
  • Second Semester Fee Deadline: 15th December
  • Late Payment Penalty: A penalty of GHS 200 will be applied for payments made after the deadline.
